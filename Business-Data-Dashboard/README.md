**Employee \& Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard**



**Tools** **Used**: Qlik Sense



**Overview**:

This dashboard analyzes sales performance, employee productivity, customer profitability, and discount effectiveness to support data-driven business decisions.



**Key** **Insights**:

• Sales and profit contribution by employees

• Order volume, sales trends, and category performance

• Customer profitability and top/bottom customers

• Geographic distribution of sales activity

• Impact of discounts on revenue and profit



**Data** **Preparation**:

• Loaded and cleaned multiple datasets including orders, customers, employees, and sales

• Built associations and data model using Qlik Sense load editor



**Features**:

• Interactive filters for date, category, city, and employee

• KPI indicators for performance tracking

• Trend, distribution, and geographic analysis visuals



**Business** **Value**:

• Evaluate workforce productivity and regional performance

• Identify high-value customers and sales drivers

• Optimize discount strategies to improve profitability



**PDF contains the dashboard in PDF format, QVF contains the in-app dashboard with real-time toggling and testing.**
